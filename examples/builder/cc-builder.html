Download Cookie Consent

This wizard will help you setup and install Cookie Consent.

To take a full advantage of this Cookie Consent feature, please visit this website on a tablet or a desktop.

1
Choose layout

Use the layout option to change the screen positioning of your cookie consent banner.

2
Choose cookie compliance

Adjust the functionality of your cookie consent banner

  • You tell your users that you use cookies, and that by continuing to use your website they accept them.

  • You tell your users that you use cookies, and give them one button to disable cookies, and another to dismiss the message.

  • You tell your users that you wish to use cookies, and give them one button to enable cookies, and another to refuse them.

5
Get the code

Just before the </head> tag, add the following code:

<!-- Begin Cookie Consent plugin by Silktide - http://silktide.com/cookieconsent --> <script type="text/javascript"> window.cookieconsent_options = {"message":"This website uses cookies to ensure you get the best experience on our website","dismiss":"Got it!","learnMore":"More info","link":null,"theme":"dark-top"}; </script> <script type="text/javascript" src="//cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/cookieconsent2/1.0.9/cookieconsent.min.js"></script> <!-- End Cookie Consent plugin -->
